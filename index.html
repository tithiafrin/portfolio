About

Working at the intersection of labour rights, social protection, and gender equity

I coordinate multi-stakeholder programmes that translate policy frameworks into ground-level change for workers — particularly in Bangladesh's ready-made garment sector, one of the world's most complex labour environments.

My work spans Employment Injury Scheme advocacy, capacity building with trade unions, psychosocial support structures, and cross-border collaboration with European labour networks. I hold a Finnish Resident Permit and am relocating to Finland to continue this work in a Nordic context.

I bring a business education background (MBS, Management) combined with field-level programme delivery — I am equally comfortable in a donor reporting meeting as in a factory floor training session.
